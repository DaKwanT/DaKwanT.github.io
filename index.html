Best food spots in Rochester!

I fell in love with grabage plates once upon a time when I went to a local spot, Nick Tahou's, after school one day. I had just got out of practice and was especially hungry, so I made sure to pile on the meatsauce. A traditional two patty, mac salad and homefry combo was more than enough to fill my apetite. I had never had a garbage plate from any other restaurant.

I had never heard of Dogtown before a few years ago. I did not discover much of Rochester while I was here as a child
so now I am making an effort to explore what it really has to offer. I discovered Dogtown a few short years ago and instantly fell in love. They have the crispiest homefries I have ever eaten, and the atmoshphere in the restaurant is so energetic! This is my go-to spot for garbage plates in Rochester!

Wegmans has Krinkle Donuts!!

Okay so most of you might not know this, but I have been working at wegmans for about six years now and I love following the decisions they make

That being said, I came back home from camp at the end of this summer and went back to work in Pittsford, only to see a brand new donut machine being put in front of the bakery!

Men and women dressed in their business suits with with wegmans nametags cliped to their bossoms crowd the machine as it does its magic I cannot believe how blessed we are here in the north east to have wegmans. I have had a krinkle donut at the beginning of almost every shift since they installed the machine here and I do not regret it at all. Not to mention it goes great with the warm cider that they offer at The Buzz Coffee Shop!
